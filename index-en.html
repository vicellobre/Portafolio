About Me

I'm a software developer with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and experience developing projects with Unity and C#. My academic background has provided me with a solid knowledge of programming, algorithmic logic, mathematics, algebra, databases, networks, and other essential fundamentals. Throughout my career, I have worked with various programming languages, including C, C++, and Python.

However, I have a special interest in game development. I have taken several specialized courses where I have acquired the knowledge and techniques necessary to design and develop complete games in Unity. These courses have allowed me to carry out various personal projects, as well as to work as a Unity developer in companies dedicated to the development of educational simulators and gamified applications.

Currently, I am still looking to grow professionally in software development, facing new challenges that allow me to solve complex problems and lead me to consolidate myself as an experienced software architect.
