About me

I'm a developer with experience developing projects in Unity and C#. I'm finishing my degree in Computer Science, in which I learned everything fundamental about programming, algorithmic logic, mathematics, algebra, databases, networks and more. Throughout my career I have done projects with various programming languages, including C, C++ and Python.

However, I'm most passionate about video game development, so I wanted to specialize in that area and I took several courses where I learned all the concepts and techniques to design and develop complete video games in Unity, thanks to these courses I was able to develop various personal projects, and also get a position as a Unity developer in a company dedicated to developing educational simulators.

Currently I continue to grow in video games development and I'm looking for new challenges in order to learn and apply more advanced technologies in Unity such as augmented reality and virtual reality.
